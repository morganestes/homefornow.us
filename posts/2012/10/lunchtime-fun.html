Lunchtime fun

October 12 2012, 5:59 PM  by Morgan Estes

Jessica had the day off for Fall Break, so she and Joanna trekked out to Long John Silver's for lunch -- where I happily joined them.

Joanna got to taste greasy chicken and greasy fish, and seemed to enjoy both. We definitely won't make this a habit for her, as we're trying to break ourselves of bad eating habits.

But for today, it made J a happy girl!
