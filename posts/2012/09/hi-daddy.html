Hi Daddy!

September 26 2012, 6:43 PM  by Morgan Estes

Usually when I get home from work or pick her up from the sitter's, Joanna grunts and speed crawls to me with a big smile on her face, but today she was already in her high chair having some dinner.

What greeting did she have for me tonight? A loud "Hi Daddy!" as I entered the kitchen! Jessica and I both heard it loud and clear and confirmed it with each other. It was a fun surprise to hear.
Considering her first clear word was "no!", repeated over and over in line at Homeland, this was a nice second.
