A New Faucet

March 16 2011, 9:37 AM  by Jessica Estes

Morgan has made lots of nice improvements in the 9 months we’ve been in our house. We haven’t document all of them, but one that I did take before and after pictures of is the sink in our guest bathroom. While we’re not crazy about the green color in the countertop, it still works so that’s not a big problem. However, the original faucet was so close to the back wall of the sink that it was difficult for me (with my pretty small hands) to get a good hand-washing in there. So, for other adults it was just a pain. Morgan looked at it and said he could switch out the faucet for a new one that would extend further into the sink and be way better to use. He was right! After a quick trip to Home Depot we had a new faucet and he installed it. It’s way better than the old one.

Jessica
