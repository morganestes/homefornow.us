Not surprising...

November 1 2010, 8:08 PM  by Jessica Estes
So, I often say that Morgan can fix anything. Tonight he proved my faith in his fix-it ability to be founded in reality yet again

Here he is after fixing a DVD saga:

The DVD player that was connected to our TV hasn't been working right, it skips. This is kind of a big deal since we don't have cable and I'm working my way through the Gilmore Girls series on DVD.

Tonight he heard the skipping and pulled out another player. It's one that we got free awhile back. Once it was connected we remembered why we haven't used it before - no remote came with it. A remote is essential to be able to watch various episodes on the disc.

Using his phone Morgan was able to find the needed codes to program a remote we already had to work with this machine. It worked!

Once again he fixed it! And, now my DVD watching can be back on track.
