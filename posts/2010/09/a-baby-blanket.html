A Baby Blanket

September 15 2010, 8:19 AM  by Jessica Estes

This one was fun to make! It’s for a friend and co-worker who loves Oklahoma Christian University, so I made it as close to the school colors as I could. It turned out great and their daughter looks real sweet in it. I love being able to make meaningful gifts for friends. It’s also cool that I can pray for the baby & my friends, the soon-to-be parents as I do the stitches. Crocheting has sure been fun for me!

 

Jessica
