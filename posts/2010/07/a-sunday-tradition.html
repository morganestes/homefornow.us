A Sunday Tradition

July 25 2010, 11:57 AM  by Jessica Estes
Sometimes it's fun to have a tradition, and Morgan and I have developed one over the last couple of years, Red Lobster for lunch on Sunday!

I think it started when we were trying to eat healthier. I found out I liked tilapia by trying it on the school cafeteria, I figured if I liked it there it was worth trying at a retaraunt. The tradition continued with some great gift cards from family!

At our healthiest we were sharing a grilled tilapia and adding a salad.

Here it is, the tilapia prepared on the wood fired grill. It's great to get it at least once a month, sometimes twice!
