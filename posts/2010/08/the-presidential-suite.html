The Presidential Suite

August 10 2010, 8:34 AM  by HomeForNow

IMG_0000.mov Watch this video

Our friend offered us a hotel in Anaheim. It ended up being the presidential suite at the Worldmark Hotel, a suite that sleeps 8 and overlooks Disneyland. It was a night of luxury for us.

It was really cool to be able to watch the Disney fireworks from our balcony!
